Integrated data. Sophisticated analysis. Easy access.

Vision comes with an extensive set of sophisticated, yet easy-to-use, investment management and analysis applications designed to meet the needs of such functional areas as:

Vision's applications are delivered directly to your users' desktops through their familiar intranet browser. What's more, you can tailor the applications, and add new ones, as your firm's business requirements dictate.

A System Designed to Meet Your Needs

Vision provides an infinitely flexible, extendible environment. As a result, your Vision portfolio management and analysis system can be customized easily to meet the needs of your firm and your investment professionals.

At the beginning, you may decide to implement Vision quickly and use the standard set of applications that come with the product. Over time, you may decide to modify the applications, add new applications, or add new data sources. Eventually, you may decide to exploit Vision's full range of capabilities as both a powerful database management engine and an object- oriented programming language.

Vision allows you to design a system that can meet your needs today and adapt to your changing needs tomorrow.

Implementing Vision is Easy

Insyte's technical consultants will work closely with you to ensure a smooth system implementation. With our clear installation notes and personal technical assistance, installing the Vision software is easy. In addition, with our well- defined startup procedures, getting your system to production may take only a matter of weeks:

Weeks 1 through 4. The goal in the first four weeks is to define and load your internal data so that portfolio managers can view their own portfolios via their desktop browsers.

Weeks 5 through 9. The goal during the second four weeks is to link your system to external data feeds, modify Vision's standard applications to meet your needs, and develop custom reports. At the end of this period, you will have a complete, functional system with all the data feeds and applications you require for rollout.

Weeks 10 through 14. The goal during this period is to put the data upload process and other necessary operational procedures into routine production, enabling you to smoothly manage the day-to-day administration of your Vision portfolio management and analysis system.
